The Guide

The Workout Plan goes by the 3 x 8 rule. Meaning, in every exercise, choose the weight you can manage 3 sets of, with 8 repetitions. As you progress on your strength journey, increase the weight to always keep to the 3 x 8 rule.

The Diet

You are what you eat...

In the strength-community there's a saying: "abs are made in the kitchen." What this literally means is that in order to benefit from your hard work in the gym you have to give your body the right building blocks. Think of it as fuel and oil for your car.


However, in the modern day and age, we have other resposibilities too. We go to work, maybe we have kids and partners we have to invest time in, we also want to have some hobbies and friends we can do fun activites with.
