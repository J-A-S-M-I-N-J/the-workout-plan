The Guide

The Workout Plan goes by the 3 x 8 rule. Meaning, in every exercise, choose the weight you can manage 3 sets of, with 8 repetitions. As you progress on your strength journey, increase the weight to always keep to the 3 x 8 rule. Carry out these workout-splits in 4 different occassions every week.

Chest & Triceps

  • Bench Press
  • Dumbbell Flyes
  • Incline Bench Press
  • Skullcrushers
  • Overhead Cable Tricep Extentions
  • Dips

Back & Biceps

  • Deadlift
  • Lat Pulldown
  • Reverse Flyes
  • Bicep Curls
  • Hammer Curls
  • Preacher Curls

Shoulders & Core

  • Shoulder Press
  • Kettlebell Upright Row
  • Shrugs
  • Decline Crunches
  • The Plank
  • V-Ups

Leg day

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Extention (Machine)
  • Leg Curl (Machine)
  • Calf Raises
